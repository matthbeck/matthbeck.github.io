Hotels

The hotel options near San Francisco State University aren't great, but there are two hotels in walking distance from campus:

  • Days Inn San Francisco At The Beach (1.8 miles from San Francisco State University, one can take MUNI bus, calm area with same restaurants a 15 minute walk away).
  • Hampton Inn San Francisco-Daly City (very close to BART, 2.1 miles from San Francisco State University, one can take MUNI bus)

There are numerous hotels in downtown San Francisco, about a 20-minute streetcar ride away from San Francisco State University (stop: 19th Ave & Holloway).
