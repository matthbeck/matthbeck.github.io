The Reciprocity Law for Dedekind Sums via the constant Ehrhart coefficient

Matthias Beck

Abstract. We obtain a new motivated proof of the reciprocity law for Dedekind sums by computing the constant coefficient of the Ehrhart polynomial for a rectangular triangle in two ways. On the one hand, the constant term is the Euler characteristic, and on the other hand it is given by a cotangent Laurent expansion.
