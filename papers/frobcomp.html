Some Experimental Results on the Frobenius Problem

Matthias Beck, David Einstein, and Shelemyahu Zacks

Abstract. We study the Frobenius problem: given relatively prime positive integers a_1, ... , a_n, find the largest value of t (the Frobenius number) such that

m_1 a_1 + ... + m_n a_n = t

has no solution in positive integers m_1, ... , m_n. Based on empirical data, we conjecture that except for some special cases the Frobenius number can be bounded from above by

sqrt( a_1 a_2 a_3 )5/4 - a_1 - a_2 - a_3.
