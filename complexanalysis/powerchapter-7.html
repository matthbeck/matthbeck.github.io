    1.

    For each of the sequences, prove convergence or divergence. If the sequence converges, find the limit.
    🔗

    (a)

    \(a_n = e^{\frac{ \pi i n} 4}\)
    🔗
    Answer.
    divergent
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    (b)

    \(a_n =\frac{(-1)^n} n\)

    Answer.
    convergent (limit \(0\))
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    (c)

    \(a_n = \cos(n)\)
    🔗
    Answer.
    divergent
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    (d)

    \(a_n = 2 - \frac{ i \, n^2 }{ 2n^2 + 1 }\)
    🔗

    convergent (limit \(2-\frac i2\))
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    (e)

    \(a_n =\sin ( \frac 1 n )\)
    🔗
    Answer.
    convergent (limit \(0\))
