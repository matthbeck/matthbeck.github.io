Corollary 6.2.6.

Suppose \(u\) and \(v\) are harmonic in the bounded region \(G\) and continuous on its closure. If \(u(z) = v(z)\) for all \(z \in \partial G\) then \(u(z) = v(z)\) for all \(z \in G\text{.}\)
