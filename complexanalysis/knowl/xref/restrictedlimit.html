Proposition 2.1.4.

Suppose \(f: G \to \C\) and \(\lim_{z\to z_0}f(z) = w_0\text{.}\) Suppose \(\widetilde G \subseteq G\) and \(z_0\) is an accumulation point of \(\widetilde G\text{.}\) If \(\widetilde f\) is the restriction of \(f\) to \(\widetilde G\text{,}\) then \(\lim_{z\to z_0} \widetilde f(z)\) exists and has the value \(w_0\text{.}\)
