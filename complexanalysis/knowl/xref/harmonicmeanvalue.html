Theorem 6.2.1.

Suppose \(u\) is harmonic in the region \(G\) and \(\overline D[w,r] \subset G\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} u(w) \ = \ \frac 1 {2 \pi} \int_0^{2 \pi} u \! \left( w + r \, e^{it} \right) \diff{t} \, \text{.} \end{equation*}
