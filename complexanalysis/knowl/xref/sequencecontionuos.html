Proposition 7.3.4.

Suppose \(G\subset\C\) and \(f_n: G \to \C\) is continuous, for each \(n \ge 1\text{.}\) If \((f_n)\) converges uniformly to \(f: G \to \C\) then \(f\) is continuous.
