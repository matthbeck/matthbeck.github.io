Definition 3.3.1.

The stereographic projection of \(\mathbb{S}^2\) to \(\Chat\) from \(N\) is the map \(\phi \colon \, \mathbb{S}^2 \to \Chat\) defined as follows. For any point \(P \in \mathbb{S}^2 \setminus \{N\}\text{,}\) as the \(z\)-coordinate of \(P\) is strictly less than 1, the line through \(N\) and \(P\) intersects \(\C\) in exactly one point, which define to be \(\phi(P)\text{.}\) We also declare that \(\phi(N) := \infty\text{.}\) FigureÂ 3.3.2 provides an illustration of this definition.
