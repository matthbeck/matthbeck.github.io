Example 1.4.4.

For \(r>0\) and \(a \in \C\text{,}\) the sets \(\left\{ z \in \C : \, |z-a| \lt r \right\} = D[a,r]\) and \(\left\{ z \in \C : \, |z-a| > r \right\}\) are open. The closed disk
\begin{equation*} \overline D[a,r] \ := \ \left\{ z \in \C : \, |z-a| \le r \right\} \end{equation*}
is an example of a closed set.
